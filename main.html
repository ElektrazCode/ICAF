Let's seed children's imagination, cultivate their creativity, and grow mutual empathy for prosperity and peace.
Imagination crystallizes the pursuit of happiness. Creativity helps one adapt to the Fourth Industrial Revolution and also advances it. When creativity is infused with empathy, "creative-empaths" can work together to transform this wounded world into a wonderous one.
For the past twenty-five years, we have served American children as their national arts organization and the world's children as their global arts organization.
